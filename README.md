E-Commerce Customer & Purchase Analytics Dashboard

Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer behavior, engagement patterns, and purchase trends in an e-commerce environment using Power BI and Python. The dashboard transforms raw customer data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations, KPI analysis, and customer segmentation.

The project helps identify factors influencing customer engagement and purchase conversion, enabling businesses to make data-driven decisions for improving customer retention, engagement, and sales performance.

⸻

Objectives

* Analyze customer behavior and purchasing patterns
* Identify factors influencing customer engagement and conversion
* Visualize customer journey stages and user interaction trends
* Perform customer segmentation using demographic and behavioral data
* Evaluate the impact of advertisements and discounts on purchases
* Develop interactive dashboards for business analytics and reporting
* Generate actionable business insights for decision-making

Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards
* Customer Journey Funnel
* Gender Distribution Analysis
* Device Usage by Age Group

* Engagement and Retention Metrics

⸻

Key Insights

* Mobile devices generate the highest customer traffic
* Highly engaged users show better purchase conversion rates
* Discounts and advertisements positively influence purchases
* Returning customers contribute significantly to overall engagement
* Low retention users exhibit higher bounce rates

⸻

Business Recommendations

* Improve mobile user experience and optimization
* Use personalized discounts and targeted marketing campaigns
* Focus on customer retention and loyalty strategies
* Improve engagement for low-retention users
* Enhance recommendation systems for better conversions

⸻

Final Story with the Dashboard

The dashboard provides a complete overview of customer behavior, engagement, and purchase trends in the e-commerce platform. It demonstrates how customer engagement, device usage, demographics, advertisements, and discounts influence purchasing behavior.

The analysis highlights that highly engaged and returning users contribute more to purchase conversions, while promotional strategies such as discounts positively impact customer buying decisions. The dashboard supports data-driven business decision-making and helps improve customer engagement, retention, and sales performance.

Conclusion

This project demonstrates how Power BI and data analytics techniques can transform raw customer data into actionable business insights. The dashboard helps businesses better understand customer behavior, optimize marketing strategies, improve user engagement, and increase purchase conversion performance.
